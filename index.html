About Me

General Info About Me

I am a 4th year student at the University of Alberta studying a Bachelors of Science in Kinesiology as well as a self-taught web developer.

I am the type of person to feel joy when helping others in their time of need. However, I am also a very practical person, and as such, I want to be able to deliver assistance in a practical form. When trying to help someone, I donâ€™t provide emotional support. I try to help them look for solutions to their problems.

This translates into my future interests.

Kinesiology: As a general health-based profession, kinesiology allows me to utilize problem-solving skills, anatomy, bio-mechanics, and much more to help others with their bodies and to fix issues pertaining to their health. Because at the end of the day, health is wealth right?

Coding: Technology is the future. And what more practical way to benefit the masses than computer science? Our advance in technology is to continue to make life easier for others. The mere notion of automating programs to do things for me gets me so excited every time!

These two subjects branch off into so many more disciplines that identifying them with only these two words is a shame. I am deeply in love with these two subjects in two different but also similar ways. One boasts knowledge in bio-medical sciences whereas the other emphasizes math and problem-solving.
